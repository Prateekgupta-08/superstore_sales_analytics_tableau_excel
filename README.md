# 📊 Superstore Sales Analytics Dashboard | Tableau

## 📌 Project Overview

This project presents an interactive business intelligence dashboard developed in **Tableau Public** using the Superstore dataset.

The objective was to transform raw sales data into meaningful business insights by designing an executive dashboard that enables stakeholders to monitor business performance, identify growth opportunities, and make data-driven decisions.

---

# 🚀 Business Problem

Retail businesses generate thousands of sales transactions across multiple regions, customer segments, and product categories.

Without a centralized reporting system, it becomes difficult to answer important business questions such as:

- Which region generates the highest sales?
- Which product categories contribute the most revenue?
- What is the current Return Rate?
- Is the company maintaining healthy profit margins?
- Which customer segment contributes the highest sales?
- What are the overall sales trends over time?

The objective was to build an interactive dashboard that allows business users to answer these questions quickly.

---

# 🎯 Objectives

- Design an executive-level KPI dashboard
- Monitor Sales, Profit Margin, and Return Rate
- Analyze performance by Region, Category, and Customer Segment
- Identify Top Performing Products
- Track Sales Trends over time
- Enable interactive business exploration using filters and parameters

# 📊 Business Insights

- Technology generated the highest sales among all categories.
- West region recorded the highest overall sales.
- Consumer segment contributed the largest share of revenue.
- Phones emerged as the highest-selling sub-category.
- Return Rate remained at approximately 5.9%.
- Profit Margin remained healthy at approximately 12.5%.
- Sales showed an overall upward trend across the reporting period.
